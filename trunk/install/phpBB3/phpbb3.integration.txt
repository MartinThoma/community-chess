1.  Have a working phpBB3 installation
2.  Follow the Community Chess installation instructions
3.  Execute phpbb.sql
4.  Copy phpbb3.php, login.wrapper.php and wrapper.inc.php to the main 
    Community Chess folder
    (login.wrapper.php and wrapper.inc.php should replace the old ones)
5.  Adjust 'forum/' in phpbb3.php if neccessary, e.g. to '../phpBB3/'
6.  Adjust constants in constants.inc.php:
     * Change USERS_TABLE      to 'phpbb_users' (or whatever your phpbb prefix is)
     * Change USER_NAME_COLUMN to 'username'
     * Change EXCLUDE_USERS_SQL to ' AND `group_id` != 6 AND '.USERS_TABLE.'.`user_id` != 1'
